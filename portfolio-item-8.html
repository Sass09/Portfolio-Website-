IMBD Movie Data Set Analaysis

Text Mining and Co-occurence Networks

This data was obtatined from Tidy Tuesday package. I visualised if the longer movie times correlate with lower ratings using ggplot. I was interested in finding out the common words in the titles of the movies which were in the Christmas category. I used the unnest_tokens to get one word per row and used anti-join function to get rid of stop words. I used the wordcloud package to construct a word cloud.

In order to see what words occur frequently with the word "Christmas", I constructed a bigram consisting of words which are adjacent to "Christmas". I first converted the dataframe to a Corpus and removed stop words using the functions from the package tm. It is important to make sure the column is character and not factor. Using unnest_tokens, I converted the movie title dataframe to two words per row. I counted the numbe of times those pairs occured. Bigraph was used from the package igraph to construct a bigram.
