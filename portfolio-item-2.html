Product & Customer Analysis

Demographics, Preferences, and Insights Driving Customer Behavior

This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of key metrics related to customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and revenue streams. Designed for decision-makers seeking to understand trends and identify actionable insights, it integrates data visualization techniques to highlight critical areas of focus for business strategy.

Customer Base: With 3,900 customers generating $233,081 in total revenue, the dashboard offers a clear view of engagement and financial performance. The average product rating stands at 3.7, signaling consistent but improvable customer satisfaction.
Demographics: Male customers represent 68% of the customer base, and the 46-55 age group spends the most ($45,619), highlighting this segment as a key driver of revenue.
Behavioral Trends: 43% of customers use promo codes, and 73% are non-subscribers, indicating untapped potential for customer retention and loyalty programs. Bi-weekly and monthly purchases are the most common frequencies.
Revenue Streams: Clothing dominates as the top category, while outerwear shows minimal traction, suggesting room for growth in product offerings.
Shipping and Payment: Free shipping is the most preferred option (17.13%), while PayPal (17.4%) and cash (17.2%) lead payment preferences, reflecting customer priorities for convenience and cost-effectiveness.
